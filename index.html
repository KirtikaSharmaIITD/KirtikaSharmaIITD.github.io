Nipun Batra is an Associate Professor in Computer Science at IIT Gandhinagar. He previously completed his postdoc at the University of Virginia and his PhD at IIIT Delhi as a TCS PhD fellow. His group, the Sustainability Lab, focuses on machine learning and sensing for computational sustainability, addressing issues like smart buildings, air quality, and wearable healthcare.

His work has received numerous accolades, including the Young Alumni Award from IIIT Delhi, Best PhD Presentation at ACM Sensys, Best Demo at ACM Buildsys, and a Best Video Nominee at ACM KDD.
